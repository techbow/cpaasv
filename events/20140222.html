The 20 folk artists from China (see the description below) have been scheduled on tour to Silicon Valley at IPAC's Spring Fair on 2/22 Saturday and 2/23 Sunday in IPAC's outdoor plaza in Orchard Farm Shopping Center. Decorated with lanterns and flags around, the outdoor booths are also available for local merchants to promote their products and services. In addition, CPAA will invite local talents to demonstrate their arts on an outdoor stage. This is an opportunity for art teachers to showcase their students and pass out their class information to recruit new students. The admission is free, and there is plenty of free parking spaces in the plaza and in the neighborhood. Here is the 2-day program:

While San Francisco and Oakland have celebrated the Chinese New Year with parade and outdoor fair for decades, here in Silicon Valley, we strive to establish the Silicon Valley Spring Festival as the largest Chinese culture event in the South Bay. Our ultimate goal is to establish the Chinese New Year a public holiday like Christmas. You are cordially invited to work together to achieve this goal.
