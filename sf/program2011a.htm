The annual Spring Festival Silicon Valley (SFSV) is a full-scale production that is a variety show to entertain the general public to share the joy of the lunar New Year. The program will include two new works by CPAA and prestigious art companies in Bay Area. The special guests from China will be the Sichuan State Performing Arts Company. The delegation will be led by its Director Yu Lan Wang (National First-Rank Dancer) and include top-notch acrobats, vocalists, magician, and comedian.

SFSV 2011 Program 硅谷春晚節目

1. Twelve Chinese Zodiacs  十二生肖迎兔年: 硅谷人拜年
      (CPAA 飛揚藝術團)

2. Little Peking Opera 《梨園俏妞妞》
      (CPAA Youth Group 飛揚少年精英團)

3. Magic 現代魔術表演《夢幻爵士》
     (Yi Yang 楊屹)

4. Folk Song & Dance Ensemble 民族歌舞大連唱
     a. My Hometown
     b. Road to Heaven
     c. The Mountain
     d. Mayila
     ( Harmony Voice of Artists 和諧之聲及灣區各舞蹈隊)

5. Folk Dance: Moonlight 舞蹈《月光》
      (Hui Zhou 周輝 )

6. Silk Road Fantasy - Persia & Uighur 絲路神奇之波斯與維吾爾
     - World premiere -
     a. Iranian Music Ensemble 波斯之音 ( Meehan Ensemble 伊朗樂隊 )
     b. Joy of the Spring 歡樂新春
         ( Meehan Ensemble 伊朗樂隊, CPAA Dancers 飛揚藝術團,
           Yong Ping Tien & Associates 加州國樂團)

     Intermission 中場休息

7. Yellow River Cantata Excerpts 黃河大合唱選段
    (Lead Singer: Dang Wei Guong 彎區各合唱團、領唱：黨偉光)

8. Skid 相聲 (Tien Gao Min)

9. The Chinese Emperor's Nightingale 中國皇帝與夜鶯 - 舞蹈、音樂劇
       -World premiere-
       (CPAA dancers 飛揚藝術團 , actors, Kung Fu masters)

10. Swan Lake on Shoulder 肩上芭蕾 (Video: Great Chinese State Circus)
        (Sichuan Acrobatics 四川遂宁雜技團)

11. Fireworks 慶新年
       (CPAA dancers 飛揚藝術團)

12. Finale 總謝幕

Note: Program subject to change without prior notice.
