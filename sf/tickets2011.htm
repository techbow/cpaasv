- Order online:
Ordering tickets online is quick, easy, and reliable via a PayPal shopping cart. We don't access or store your sensitive financial information because it is securely processed by paypal. There is no additional charge for online ordering. If you choose to have the tickets mail to you, there will be a $1 charge for the shipping (Mail option is no longer available starting January 15, 2011).

There is 40% discount for children between 4 and 12 and seniors over 60. Children under 3 are not recommended to attend the performance. The group rate is 10% off for 10+ tickets and 20% off for 40+ tickets. Additional 5% discount to a ticket if purchased before December 15, 2010. Great for Christmas & New Year gifts. For more information, please refer to the FAQ.
