Built on the success of Spring Festival Silicon Valley (SFSV) 2009, the sequel SFSV 2010, will be presented by CPAA at a higher level, as an international event. Through Live Video Streaming, a cutting edge technology, the joy of this most joyous and important Chinese festival is shared with the world.

Starting from Chinese new year eve, Chinese spring festivities last for several weeks. The tradition can be traced back 8,000 years, to when the Chinese tribesmen settled down to grow crops. Spring was the right season to plan for a bumper harvest. Nowadays, it has become a spiritual rite for new hope, new luck, new wealth, and all good wishes. The spring celebration has indeed a universal and timeless value. Even now, in the 21st Century, 25% of humanity, representing one quarter of the world market, is celebrating the spring

CPAA, known for its creativity in the production of high artistic spectacles, will present the SFSV 2010 on February 20, 2010 in Silicon Valley, the world’s hi-tech center and a cosmopolitan of diverse cultures. Co-sponsored by the City of San Jose, international media, and private corporations, the SFSV 2010 will be broadcasted to four corners of the world through a new technology called Live Video Streaming through internet to allow people of all ages from all walks of life, with different cultural backgrounds, to share the joy with the audience in the theater.

The SFSV 2010 is a sensational east-meets-west extravaganza. It not only showcases spectacular Chinese kung fu, dances, and acrobatics, but also international-famous vocalist from Afghanistan, ballet dancers from Cleveland Ballet, San Jose Taiko, and a guest star from China. Through artistic expression, SFSV 2010  celebrates America's diversity and inclusiveness, accepting diverse cultures as integral parts of the American society, that make America great and strong.

飛揚藝術團繼《2009硅谷春節聯歡晚會》取得成功後更上一層樓，將推出國際盛會《2010硅谷春晚》，並通過最新科技 Live Video Streaming 与全球同享此一最歡樂和最重要的中華節日。

慶祝春節這一傳統可上溯八千年，自華夏部落定居開始建立農業社會時期。一年之計在於春，而今，春節已成為中華民族充滿著希望的節日，蘊含了萬事更新，新好運，新財富，以及一切美好的祝愿與禮慶，慶祝春節確實是具有普世同慶之恆久價值。即在二十一世紀的此刻，全世界百分之二十五的人口，代表了全球四分之一的市場，都在歡慶春節。

向以創新與舉辦高水平演出著稱的飛揚藝術團將在虎年二月二十日，在世界高科技中心的硅谷，也是多元文化之國際大都會，邀請各界如聖荷西市政府，國際媒體，商業公司等等共同舉辦2010硅谷春節晚會，並通過最尖端的傳媒技術廣播到全世界，讓全世界觀眾如置身於劇院中分享節日的歡樂。

《2010硅谷春晚》是一台東西文化融合的輝煌巨制，它不僅僅展現了有數千年曆史的中華藝術，如戲劇、武術、歌舞、雜技等。更包括了國際知名藝術家們的才藝。通過多姿多彩的演出表述了美國的包容性，正是河海不捐細流，使美國成為全球文化之大熔爐，造就了這才不過三百年曆史、強盛和偉大的國家。
