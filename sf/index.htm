Welcome to the 4th Spring Festival Silicon Valley (SFSV) on February 4, 2012 at the San Jose Center for the Performing Arts. 2012 marks CPAA's 20th Anniversary as well as the Year of the Dragon in the Chinese calendar. This is a very rare triple-celebration in the 21st Century. To celebrate this special occasion, CPAA together with 200 artists will present a sensational East-meets-West extravaganza featuring the spectacular The Legend of the Dragon King and New Year Celebration. We thank Honorable Assemblymember Paul Fong and TV anchorwoman Diana Ding to host this performance. We hope the Dragon King will bring you all the luck and prosperity to your business.

Starting from Chinese New Year's Eve, spring festivities such as parades, parties, and visiting friends last for several weeks. The tradition can be traced back 8,000 years, to when the Chinese tribesmen settled down to grow crops. Spring was determined as the right season to plan for a bumper harvest. This tradition was later introduced to other Asians like Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese, many centuries ago. Now in the 21st Century, one-quarter of humanity is celebrating the spring to hope for a better future. As one of the most ancient legacy in human history, the Spring Festival has indeed a universal and timeless value.

CPAA, known for its creativity in the production of high artistic spectacles, presents the 4th SFSV, a variety show of dance, music, kung fu, magic, acrobatics, and the spectacular fluorescent 60' Dragon to entertain audiences of all ages and different cultural backgrounds. Our goal is to establish the Asian Spring Festival similar to Christmas, joy to the world. We are grateful to many sponsors including City of San Jose, Marchese Family Foundation, San Jose Mercury News, World Channel, DingDing TV, 99People, private corporations, individuals, and over 20 community organizations to build this cultural high point in the Bay Area. Through artistic expression, the Spring Festival Silicon Valley truly celebrates the Asian New Year as well as America's diversity and inclusiveness that make America great and strong.

龍年大慶及飛揚廿週年紀念盛大公演

飛揚藝術團自2009年以來每年舉办《硅谷春晚》与全球华人和其他亚裔共享这一个最重要的中华节日。 2012年也适逢飞扬艺术团二十周年大庆，也是二十一世纪之首届龙年。为庆祝巧合的三盛会，飞扬与湾区众多的艺术团体200多演员共同推出一台辉煌巨制。分《庆新春》和《龙的传说》两大内容，这台《第四届硅谷春晚》定于2012年2月4日在硅谷的表演艺术中心盛大公演，我们非常感谢加洲议员方文忠及著名节目主持人丁维平共同担任司仪，并得到圣荷西市政府，国际媒体，各商家及许多社团的赞助，共同举办《第四届硅谷春晚》展现了有数千年历史的中华文化艺术，有戲劇、武術、歌舞、雜技，魔术等，老少咸宜，欢度春节。

慶祝春節這一傳統可上溯八千年，自華夏部落定居開始建立農業社會時期，认定一年之計在於春。而今，全世界百分之二十五的人口都在歡慶春節。春節已成為中華民族充滿著希望的節日，蘊含了萬事更新，以及一切美好的祝愿與禮慶，慶祝春節確實是具有普世同慶之恆久價值。希望 2012 年的龙年为大家带来新好運和新財富。
